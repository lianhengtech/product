Generation AI

Generation AI is an advanced solution for generating 3D mesh automatically in Blender. With the power of cutting-edge AI, it can generate images and mesh corresponding to different styles of images with minimal effort.

Key

Features

Quick 3D Generation

Automatically generates 3D meshs from images, reducing manual work.

Mesh Material Repainting

Generate mesh with different texture based on different style images of the same object.

Upscale Texture

Generates higher resolution texture with added detail and clarity.
